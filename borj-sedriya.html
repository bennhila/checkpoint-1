bni mtir

Beni M'Tir or Beni Metir (Arabic: بني مطير) is a village in northwestern Tunisia located in the mountainous region of Kroumirie a few kilometers from Ain Draham. The city is located on the territory of the confederation of Berber tribes of the Kroumirs. Located at an altitude of 650 meters3 and attached to the governorate of Jendouba, Beni M'Tir is the least populated of the country's municipalities with a population of 784 inhabitants in 20142, mainly made up of young people, living mainly from jobs provided by the Tunisian Society of electricity and gas and the National Water Exploitation and Distribution Company 3. It is attached to the delegation of Fernana.
